Lamb Waves:

Lamb Waves are surface guided waves which travel in thin structure at long distance without attenuation. They travel in Radial in-plane motion i.e. Symmetric Mode and Out of plane motion i.e. Anti-symmetric Mode as shown in fig.(a) & fig.(b) respectively.

Their velocity depends on product of wave frequency and structure thickness. A wide range of Phase Velocity and Group Velocity are available as per mode shape of thin structure as shown in fig.(c) & fig.(d) respectively.
